**Location:** Profile → Bottom of page (after Save button)

- Confirmation dialog prevents accidental resets

### 3. 📊 **Quick Stats Cards** (Dashboard)

**Location:** Dashboard → Below FSI card, above Financial Summary

**3 Beautiful Stat Cards:**

#### Card 1: Obligations Count
- 📝 Icon: List
- Shows: Number of active obligations
- Color: Blue
- Example: "3 Obligations"

#### Card 2: Savings Rate
- 📈 Icon: Trending Up
- Shows: Percentage of income saved
- Color: Green
- Formula: (Disposable Income / Monthly Income) × 100
- Example: "+60% Savings Rate"

#### Card 3: Coverage Ratio
- 🛡️ Icon: Shield
- Shows: How many months of obligations your emergency fund covers
- Color: Purple
- Formula: Emergency Savings / Total Obligations
- Example: "10x Coverage"

**Why it's USEFUL:**
- 👀 **Quick glance** - Instant financial health overview
- 📊 **Key metrics** - Most important numbers at top
- 🎯 **Actionable** - Identifies areas to improve
- 🎨 **Beautiful** - Clean card design with icons
